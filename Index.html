Intro

Astrid Klien, Founder of German Shepherd Haven-Adoption USA In 2015, I embarked on a mission to make a difference in the lives of German Shepherds in need. With a passion for these intelligent, loyal, and loving animals, I founded German Shepherd Haven-Adoption USA. What started as a local endeavor has since grown into a global organization, dedicated to finding forever homes for German Shepherds around the world. Our mission is simple: to provide a safe haven for German Shepherds in need and match them with loving families who will cherish them. We believe that every dog deserves a second chance at happiness, and we're committed to making that a reality. Today, German Shepherd Haven-Adoption USA has a network of dedicated volunteers and foster families who work tirelessly to care for our furry friends. We have a range of dogs available for adoption, from playful puppies to gentle seniors, each with their unique personality and charm. Our adoption process is designed to ensure that every dog finds the perfect match. We take the time to get to know each dog's personality, needs, and quirks, and match them with families who will provide the love, care, and attention they deserve. Whether you're a seasoned dog owner or a first-time adopter, we invite you to join our community of German Shepherd lovers. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of these incredible animals and find them the forever homes they deserve. If you're interested in learning more about our adoption process or would like to meet our available dogs, please don't hesitate to reach out.
